Transformations use the letter T
____________________________________________________________________________


  
TRafo transl

Definition of a Translation (Move)
   (Under the Modify functions)

T = TRA DirectionOfTranslation [VectorLength]
   Direction Of Translation

Example:
T20 = DR VAL (100)
Translate # Z in the direction of 100 mm




  
TRafo rot        ROT

Definition of a Rotation
   (Under the Modify functions)

T = ROT Axis RotationAngle
   Axis: point and vector ("P20 DZ") or Line ...
   Rotation angle in degrees

Example:
T20 = ROT L20 ANG (30)
# Defines a 30-Grad-Rotation of the Line L20
